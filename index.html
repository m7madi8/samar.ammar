About Us

Samar Ammar Interior Designer is a leading design studio in Palestine, specializing in creating exceptional living and working spaces. With over 8 years of experience, we combine creativity, functionality, and sustainability to deliver designs that exceed expectations.

CEO Message

We believe that architecture is not just about buildings, but about experiences. Every space we design tells a story, carefully blending aesthetics with function. Our team ensures each design improves quality of life, respects the environment, and reflects the identity of its users.

From concept to completion, we are committed to delivering innovative solutions that transform spaces into inspiring environments. Our passion for design drives us to create timeless, functional, and beautiful spaces that our clients love to call home.

Interior Design

Transform your living spaces with our comprehensive interior design services. We specialize in creating functional, beautiful, and personalized interiors that reflect your lifestyle and preferences. From concept development to final installation, we handle every aspect of your project with meticulous attention to detail.

Landscape Design

Create stunning outdoor environments that seamlessly blend with your architecture. Our landscape design services focus on creating functional, sustainable, and visually appealing outdoor spaces that enhance your property's value and provide a perfect setting for relaxation and entertainment.

Exterior Design

Make a lasting impression with our exterior design services. We create stunning building facades that combine aesthetic appeal with structural integrity. Our designs consider local climate, materials, and architectural context to create exteriors that stand the test of time while enhancing your property's curb appeal.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive interior design, exterior design, and landscape design services. This includes space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, lighting design, and complete project management.

How long does a typical project take?

Project duration varies depending on scope and complexity. Small projects may take 2-4 weeks, while larger projects can take 2-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you work with specific budgets?

Yes, we work with various budgets and always provide transparent pricing. We offer different service packages to accommodate different budget ranges while maintaining quality standards.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the West Bank area including Ramallah, Bethlehem, Nablus, and surrounding areas. We also take on projects in other Palestinian cities based on availability.

Do you provide 3D renderings?

Yes, we provide detailed 3D renderings and visualizations to help you visualize the final design before implementation. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the concept.
